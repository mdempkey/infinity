## Product Vision
This website will be a yelp-style website for Star Wars-themed attractions at amusement parks worldwide. Users can discover, view on an interactive map, rate, and review rides, restaurants, shops, and experiences at Star Wars venues globally
## Core Features (MVP)
- Interactive map view (Google Earth) with pins for all attractions worldwide
- Attraction detail pages including name, park, location, category, description, and photos
- Star Ratings (1-5) and written reviews per attraction
- User Accounts: register, login, manage own reviews
- Browse and filter by park, category, rating
- Full-text search across attraction names and reviews
## Attraction Categories
- Rides and Experiences (ie Millennium Falcon, Rise of the Resistance)
- Restaurants & Cafes (ie Docking Bay 7 Food and Cargo)
- Shops & Merchandise (ie Dok-Ondar’s Den of Antiquities, Savi’s Workshop)
- Shows & Entertainment (ie meet-and-greets, live performances)
## Parks in Scope
- Star Wars: Galaxy’s Edge (Disneyland, Anaheim CA)
- Star Wars: Galaxy’s Edge (Walt Disney World, Orlando FL)
- Star Wars: Galaxy’s Edge (Disneyland Paris)
- Star Wars Launch Bay (Hollywood Studios, Disney World
